Subway Map Tileset

For my second lab in GEOG 572, I created a custom tileset in Mapbox Studio after an aesthetic I particularly enjoy: retro tube-style subway maps. Creating a tileset from scratch was difficult, and required me to completely deconstruct this aesthetic into its basic components, paying attention to minute details like color, texture, and linework. I then explained the creation of my design with a scrollmap, using HTML and CSS code. Check out a fun interactive version of my tileset at the bottom of this scrollmap!
